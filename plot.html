Regular Show

Plot

  • Regular show is about two workers taking a job at a park. During their time at the park they will deal with promblems that will threaten their jobs and the destruction of the park.

Genre

  • Regular show is a cartoon made by Cartoon Network

Characters

  • In Regular Show the two main characters are Mordecai and Rigby. They also become friends with the other workers such as Skips Pops Muscleman and Hi-Five Ghost
